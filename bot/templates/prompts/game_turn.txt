=== DICE ROLLS — USE SPARINGLY ===
ONLY request a skill check or saving throw when ALL of these are true:
1. The player explicitly ATTEMPTS something difficult or uncertain
2. Failure would have meaningful consequences
3. It's not something anyone could do trivially

DO NOT roll for: walking, looking around, listening to surroundings, opening an unlocked door, speaking to someone friendly, basic movement, getting out of a vehicle, entering a building, casual observation, asking for directions, reading a sign.
DO roll for: picking locks, sneaking past guards, persuading a HOSTILE or SUSPICIOUS NPC, climbing a sheer wall, detecting a HIDDEN trap/secret, resisting poison, intimidating someone, lifting something heavy, calming a scared animal.

When in doubt: NO ROLL. Let the player succeed at basic actions.
